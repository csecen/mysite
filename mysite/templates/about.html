About Me

Hi! My name is Haley and I am a book lover. I got my degree in Chemical Engineering and always knew I would have a STEM career. But while I was at work one day, one of my coworkers was asking about my passions – and recording information on biological samples all day was absolutely not it. But put on the spot, I couldn’t really come up with my passion.

Later that day, I saw one of my favorite authors updated the title of her next book – and it launched me into a discussion about cliff hangers and book release schedules and an in depth look at the synopsis provided on Amazon. And of course, it clicked – here was my passion. For as long as I can remember, I have always loved to read. I will wake up at midnight on release days, cry along with the characters I’m reading about, day dream about book boyfriends that I wish weren’t fictional, and savor over those happily ever afters.

So to spare my poor coworkers who have no idea what I’m talking about 90% of the time, I decided to start this blog, where I can share my thoughts with fellow book lovers. Truly, I think my goal is to one day be an author, to have others talking about my own books. But that’s still a long ways away and for now, please enjoy my musings (and likely rambles)!
